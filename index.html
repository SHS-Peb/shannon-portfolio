About Me

Hi, I’m Shannon

I’m a Sydney-based developer with a passion for building things that make life easier, cleaner, and more connected. In pursuit of this, I have completed the Full Stack Coding Bootcamp with the University of Sydney and have continued expanding my skills ever since. Along the way, I’ve attended key industry events such as CLG Presents: The Insider with Macquarie and Code Like a Girl x TikTok, gaining valuable insights into the tech industry and the people shaping it.

Always Learning, Always Growing

What draws me to coding is the constant opportunity to learn. I am always learning new skills by pursuing personal projects, as highlighted in my blog, where I explore new technologies and take on challenges that push me to grow.

I’m passionate about creating clean, functional designs while always looking for ways to improve, refactor, and grow.

Technical Skills

  • Front-End: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React
  • Back-End: Node.js, Express.js, RESTful APIs
  • Databases: MongoDB, MySQL
  • Version Control: Git, GitHub
  • Deployment: Heroku, Netlify

More Than Just Code

Outside of coding, I’m passionate about languages and how we connect with one another — whether that’s through learning new spoken languages or the universal language of technology. I’m also a strong advocate for mental health awareness and believe that empathy and understanding are just as important in tech as technical skills.

Ready for What’s Next

I’m excited to bring my skills, curiosity, and passion for thoughtful problem-solving into a professional setting. Whether collaborating on projects or building something from the ground up, I’m ready to grow with a team that values both the human and technical side of development.
